Take part in the {{ serviceName }}

This important study looks at household borrowing, saving, and retirement plans. Your answers help us understand what's happening across the UK, informing decisions on things like affordable homes and what support people need for their retirement.

This study will ask questions about anyone in the household. It is important that everyine in your household sees the letters and leaflets we send you.

Your answers will help shape the future of the UK.

The information you give us is protected by law and is treated as confidential.

To thank you for your time...

We have included a £10 e-voucher. A £20 voucher will also be sent to each adult who completes the survey online.

The survey is split into parts. You can complete it all in one go, or save and come back. How long it takes depends on your answers, but for most people it is between 30-40 minutes.
